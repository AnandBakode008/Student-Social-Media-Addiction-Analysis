# Student Social Media Addiction Analysis

## Overview
Analysis of social media usage patterns and their effects on academic performance,
mental health, and sleep among **5,000 students** across multiple countries.

## Dataset
- 5,000 students | 8 platforms | Multiple countries
- Platforms: Instagram, Snapchat, WhatsApp, TikTok, Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, LINE

## Key Findings
- 67% of students report social media negatively affects their academics
- Average daily usage: **6.53 hrs/day**
- Average addiction score: **5.54/10**
- High School students have the highest addiction score (5.64)
- Mexico has the highest average addiction score globally
